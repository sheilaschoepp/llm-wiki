# Real committee

- **kind**: real-people
- **summary**: Your actual examiners — the named faculty on your candidacy/thesis committee. Ships with no members (only a `_TEMPLATE`): add one profile per member under `profiles/` (see the skill README, "Creating a real committee profile").
- **members**: every profile in `profiles/` (add your own; a leading-underscore `_TEMPLATE.md` is a scaffold, not a member).
- **question source**: the event prep companion in this folder (`<event>-prep.md`, e.g. `candidacy-prep.md`), when present. Profiles give each member's lens (neutral facts); the prep file gives questioning style and seed questions.
- **fabrication rule**: real people — ground every question in the member's real profile and what the document actually says. Never invent a member's paper, position, or stance.
- **profile shape**: neutral ground-truth facts only (Identity, Research focus, Key themes, Selected recent works, Sources). Inferred "what they probe" content lives in the prep file, not the profile. User-maintained — do not rewrite a profile without being asked.
